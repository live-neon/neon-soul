What is NEON-SOUL?

Your agent's soul grows from its own experience. NEON-SOUL reads your agent's interactions, finds the patterns it keeps repeating, and compresses them into identity. No more guessing at personality. No more hand-writing "be helpful and honest" when the agent didn't learn those words.

The Old Way

You write a SOUL.md by hand. You guess what your agent should value. The personality is static. It never changes unless you change it. Your agent is whoever you told it to be.

The NEON-SOUL Way

Your agent interacts with users. Memory files accumulate. NEON-SOUL reads those interactions, finds patterns, and surfaces the values your agent actually demonstrates. When the same behavior shows up three times, it becomes a core belief. The soul writes itself.

Why CJK?

Those aren't decorative characters. CJK (Chinese-Japanese-Korean) characters are among the densest writing systems on earth. A single character like Ë™† (honesty/sincerity) encodes what takes an entire English sentence. Combined with emoji indicators and mathematical notation, one line like üíé Ë™†: honesty > performance carries the same semantic weight as a full paragraph of prose.

This is how NEON-SOUL achieves 10x compression. Not by removing meaning, but by using a writing system that packs more meaning per token.
